Redesign is not an event - it is a process. Redesign never ends - these are steps taken to keep the user experience relevant. Today, when preparing a product, it is very difficult to set up a precise process and always follow it. We need to be flexible and adapt quickly. We need to know for sure what the resulting product will look like. However, users do not like the changes. It doesn't matter how comfortable and easy the new design is, they are used to the old interface and now they have to learn something new. The good news is that people get used to it in a short time, even if they don't like changes and redesigns. In the first few weeks, they face difficulties, but everything goes smoothly. Mobile Banking is constantly evolving around the world and is becoming an integral part of your smartphone. We present to you the uBank by #Unibank application, which we have been working on and continuing to develop for months, as a result of which we have undergone major changes, keeping pace with world practices, standards and trends.
